About

I'm Omkar Bhalerao, a current senior (class of 2020) at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology. This website is for Voice Identifier, a project I am researching in the Computer Systems Lab.

Voice Identifier is an online service that aims to classify people based solely on characteristics of their voice. It is a type of speaker recognition system.

Currently, to classify the audio I use a feedforward neural network. My model takes featuers from the discrete Fourier transform and the power spectral density of the audio file. However, my model has not been very effective in classifying audio, so I am currently researching alternate models.

This site allows you to test my model in action. After a person speaks into their microphone, my model will analyze their audio and output a classification prediction.
